Hello, I'm Alexandra-Maria, the Student 2 of the group. I am responsible for the Website Style (Template and Global CSS), the Homepage, the Table and the Navigation Bar. Although the only requirements were to build the website in HTML and CSS, I have also implemented JavaScript to make the site look and feel more professional and real. I have completed everything out of the required tasks. I have successfully implemented and sent my teammates the website style, I have integrated it in all my files and made sure that everything was working as intended.

Technical Description

As Student 2, the Website Style and the Homepage were the most fun parts to do. I spent the longest time on my Homepage, trying to carefully perfect it in every way and add details to it. I really enjoyed my first ever experience with JavaScript, making different animations while scrolling and attempting a hamburger menu on the navigation bar. I took as inspiration the Apple website for the animations and the theme. The colour scheme is inspired by people and nature, as the topic we are covering is 'good health and wellbeing'. The table was really easy for me to do, and I enjoyed styling it a lot. It's a very minimalistic and elegant design, as my target was for it to be easily readable and to be attractive by its simplicity.

My approach to website development centered on establishing a robust and adaptable structure that would facilitate a seamless user experience. To achieve this, I implemented a comprehensive template that served as the backbone of the entire site. This template included a persistent navigation bar, strategically positioned to provide users with intuitive access to all key sections of the website, regardless of their current location. The footer, another integral component of the template, was designed to house essential information, such as contact details, copyright notices, and supplementary links, ensuring that users could easily access these resources from any page. The home page, acting as the primary point of entry, was meticulously crafted to capture the visitor's attention and convey the website's core message. I prioritized a visually appealing design, incorporating elements such as high-quality images, concise text, and strategically placed calls to action. To effectively present data, I utilized HTML tables, focusing on clear formatting and logical organization to enhance readability. CSS was employed extensively to refine the visual presentation of the website. I implemented a consistent color palette, carefully selecting hues that conveyed the desired tone and aesthetic. Typography was also a key consideration, with font choices and sizes carefully selected to ensure readability and maintain a professional appearance. Layouts were created using modern CSS techniques, including flexbox and grid, which allowed for precise control over element positioning and responsiveness across various screen sizes. Semantic HTML tags, such as nav, footer, article, and section, were employed to create a logical and accessible structure, enhancing both search engine optimization and user experience. The use of CSS variables allowed for easy maintenance and changes to the overall site style. This methodical approach ensured a website that was not only visually pleasing but also functionally sound and easily navigable.
